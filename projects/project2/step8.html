Animating a Character in After Effects

Step 8. Apply to other parts.

Now you get to play! With these steps you can do a bunch of other stuff to the character. Keyframes apply to other properties such as scale (shortcut S), rotation (R), and opacity (T.) This can allow for a lot of expressive faces for your character.

Now you have the basic knowledge to start animating in After Effects! GET TO WORK.
